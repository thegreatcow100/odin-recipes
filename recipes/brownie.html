Chocolate Brownies

The recipe creates a pan of delicious brownies that will surely knock your socks off. Whether they are an after school treat for your kids or a nice dessert for next to the campfire, these chocolate brownies are all you need to bring a smile on a loved one's face

Ingredients

Brownie Mix

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Frosting

  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of sugar

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

  2. Step 2

    Melt 1/2 cup butter in a large saucepan. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

  3. Step 3

    Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool briefly before frosting.

  4. (Optional) Step 4

    To make the frosting: Combine softened butter, sugar, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract in a bowl. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
